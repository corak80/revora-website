Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Revora, from application to clinical outcomes.

Revora is a simple, cost-effective 0.8% hyaluronic acid (HA) gel formulation designed specifically for veterinary dental applications. It comes in a pre-filled 1ml luer-lock syringe with blunt-nosed applicator tips for convenient and precise delivery directly to oral tissues. Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring biomaterial proven safe and effective in both humans and canines for a variety of dental indications.
Revora's hyaluronic acid gel works through multiple mechanisms: it reduces inflammation and soothes irritated gum tissue, supports the regeneration of healthy gum tissue including bone and periodontal ligament, and establishes a microbial barrier that protects teeth from plaque and tartar buildup. HA is extremely hydrophilic — absorbing up to 1,000× its weight in water — and increases in viscosity when it contacts blood or saliva, forming a stable protective matrix over the treatment site.
Revora is indicated for a wide range of veterinary dental procedures, including: routine dental prophylaxis (even for healthy teeth), post-extraction wound management, gingival surgery support, treatment of furcational periodontal disease, and gingival recession cases. It facilitates tissue recovery after both routine and complex dental procedures.
